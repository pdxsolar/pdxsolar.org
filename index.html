PDX Solar

Promoting independent, affordable, libre solar energy in Portland, Oregon

Welcome

PDX Solar is a community initiative dedicated to making solar energy accessible to everyone in Portland, Oregon. We believe in the power of independent, affordable, and libre (free/open) solar solutions.

Our Mission

We share free resources, knowledge, and support to help Portlanders take control of their energy future through solar power. Whether you're just getting started or looking to expand your solar setup, we're here to help.

Get Involved

Join our community to access free resources, connect with other solar enthusiasts, and learn about independent solar solutions that work for Portland's unique climate and conditions.

We're an all-volunteer membership organization. PDX Solar is a registered nonprofit public benefit corporation in Portland, Oregon. hellopdxsolar@gmail.com    @pdxsolar.org
